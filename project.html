Artificial Intelligence in Economics | Fall 2024

Project Overview

This project explores how artificial intelligence tools can support managerial economics, focusing on building data-driven insights for a real or simulated business problem.

I combine theoretical concepts from the course with hands-on implementation, using AI tools to organize information, analyze data, and generate decision-focused recommendations.

The final deliverables include a presentation deck and a written report that summarize the methodology, findings, limitations, and potential next steps for applying AI in this context.

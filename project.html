Final Project Summary

This project analyzes the U.S. sports sponsorship industry using AI-supported research methods. The report examines industry growth trends, major sponsorship drivers such as digital assets and NIL, and the expanding role of data and artificial intelligence in sponsorship valuation and strategy. The project also reflects on how AI can support decision-making while emphasizing the importance of human judgment in sports business contexts.
